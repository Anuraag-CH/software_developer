How to be a good Software Developer

Academics

Make sure you finish your coursework with decent grades.

Data Structures and Algorithms

Along with course work special emphasis must be laid on learning Various Data structures and algorithms.

Coding Competetions

Try and take part in various coding competetions.Below are few websites where you can take part.

    • Leetcode
      Coding Ninjas
      HackerRank
      Hackerearth
      Codeforces

    • Personal Projects

    Try and learn different skills and the best way to showcase those skills is to make different projects that highlight your knowledge and deepen your understanding of the technology used.

    Opensource Contributions

    Opensource contributions will help a lot in understanding the structure of the repositories. It will also help understand the different tools and technologies used.It will be a great learning curve.

    Certifications

    Getting certified in a particular tool or technology will help the potential employer understand your skill.
